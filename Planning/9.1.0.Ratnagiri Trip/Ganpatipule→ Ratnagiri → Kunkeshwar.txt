            |************************ Ratnagiri Trip *******************|

------------------------------ Day 1 : -----------------------
Day 1: Shirur → Ganpatipule

🚗 Distance: ~400 km | ⏱ Travel: ~9 / 10 hrs

03:00 am – Start from Shirur
Carry breakfast/snacks or stop on the Pune–Satara highway.

09:00 am – Breakfast stop (Satara or Karad).

12:00 pm – Visit Ganpatipule Temple (Swayambhu Ganpati) 
→ Peaceful darshan, right on the beach / Ganpatipule Beach

03:00 pm – Resort 
→ Check-in hotel/resort, have lunch.

04:30 pm – Visit Aare Ware Beach [21 min / 10.9 km]
Just 10 mins away, scenic drive, less crowded.

07:00 pm – Return to hotel
Evening snacks/dinner, night stay at Ganpatipule.

------------------------------ Day 2 : -----------------------

Day 2: Ganpatipule → Ratnagiri (Stay overnight in Ratnagiri)

🚗 Distance: ~45 km | ⏱ Travel: ~1 hr 15 min

06:30 am – Wake up & morning beach walk at Ganpatipule (peaceful time).

08:00 am – Breakfast at hotel.

09:00 am – Leave Ganpatipule → Ratnagiri (~1 hr 15 min).

10:30 am – Visit Ratnadurg Fort (amazing sea views, lighthouse nearby).

12:00 pm – Visit Thibaw Palace (Burmese King’s historic palace).

01:00 pm – Lunch in Ratnagiri (try Malvani/Konkani thali).

02:00 pm – Relax at Bhatye Beach or Mandvi Beach.

04:00 pm – Optional: Visit Marine Aquarium & Museum (great for kids/family).

06:00 pm – Sunset at Bhatye Beach.

07:30 pm – Dinner & overnight stay in Ratnagiri.

------------------------------ Day 3 : -----------------------
Day 3: Ratnagiri → Kunkeshwar → Return Home

🚗 Distance: ~85 km (Ratnagiri → Kunkeshwar) | ⏱ Travel: ~2 hrs

06:30 am – Early wake up & breakfast in Ratnagiri.

07:30 am – Drive to Kunkeshwar (~2 hrs).

09:30 am – Visit Kunkeshwar Shiva Temple (beautiful temple on the beach).

10:30 am – Enjoy Kunkeshwar Beach – peaceful, clean, less crowded.

12:00 pm – Early lunch / snacks at Devgad–Kunkeshwar area (try mango products if in season 🥭).

01:00 pm – Start return journey home.
