 |******************** Hampi Karnataka Trip (4-Day Plan from Pune) ********************|


|------------------------ Day 1 – Pune → Hampi ------------------------|
04 : 30 AM – Leave Pune (keep snacks + water).
07 : 30 AM – Breakfast @ Kolhapur (Hotel Opal / Padma Guest House).
12 : 30 PM – Lunch @ Hubli – Clarks Inn / Kamat Upachar.
05 : 30 PM – Reach Hospet / Hampi & check-in.

Evening:

Short walk near Virupaksha Temple Gopuram (lit at night).

Dinner @ Mango Tree Café / Chillout Café.

Stay: Hospet (Clarks Inn / Hampi Boulders Resort / Gopi Guest House).

|------------------------- Day 2 – Hampi Heritage Exploration ------------------------|

05 : 45 AM – Sunrise at Hemakuta Hill / Matanga Hill.
07 : 30 AM – Breakfast @ Hampi Bazaar café.

Morning Circuit – Sacred Center:

Virupaksha Temple

Hemakuta Group of Temples

Vittala Temple & Stone Chariot (₹20 buggy ride)

Coracle Ride on Tungabhadra River (optional)

12 : 30 PM – Lunch @ Mango Tree / Laughing Buddha Café.
01 : 30 PM – 02 : 15 PM – Rest at hotel.

Afternoon – Royal Center:

Mahanavami Dibba & Royal Enclosure

Lotus Mahal & Elephant Stables

Queen’s Bath

Hazara Rama Temple

05 : 30 PM – Sunset at Anjanadri Hill (Hanuman Birthplace).
07 : 30 PM – Dinner & rest.

|------------------------ Day 3 ------------------------|

Use this day for relaxed local and natural sightseeing nearby.

07 : 00 AM – Breakfast.
08 : 00 AM – 11 : 00 AM – Visit Anegundi Village (across river):

Old-world Hampi houses & Hanuman temple paintings.

Sanapur Lake → Coracle ride & swimming spot (photography heaven).

11 : 30 AM – 01 : 00 PM – Visit Tungabhadra Dam & Garden (Hospet) – optional laser show in evening.

01 : 00 PM – 02 : 00 PM – Lunch in Hospet (@ Naveen Regency or Kamat Hotel).
03 : 00 PM – 05 : 00 PM – Relax @ hotel pool or local market for souvenirs (stone carvings).
05 : 30 PM – Sunset from Hampi Boulders Viewpoint or Hemakuta Hill.
08 : 00 PM – Dinner & prepare for return.

Stay: Same hotel (Hospet / Hampi).

|------------------------Day 4 – Hampi → Pune------------------------|

To reach Pune around 2 PM, start early.

03 : 30 AM – Check-out and depart Hampi.
07 : 30 AM – Breakfast @ Hubli (Kamat Upachar).
11 : 00 AM – Short halt @ Kolhapur for tea/snack.
01 : 30 – 02 : 00 PM – Reach Pune.
